Our story

Building beautiful spaces since 2000

EA Renovations is a family-driven renovation business based in Benoni, dedicated to turning everyday spaces into functional and modern environments.

Who we are

A trusted partner for every phase

For over 25 years, EA Renovations has been transforming homes and commercial spaces across Gauteng. What began as a small family operation has grown into a full-service renovation and fit-out company, trusted by hundreds of homeowners, developers, and business owners.

We specialize in interior renovations, design and build, custom joinery, tiling, paving, repairs, and solar installations — providing end-to-end project management with a strong focus on quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction.

Our promise is simple: clear pricing, realistic timelines, and craftsmanship we stand behind.
